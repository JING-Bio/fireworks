What is it?
A genome-wide, hierarchical visualisation of Reactome pathways in a space-filling graph.
What is its aim?
Visualisation of genome-scale pathway analysis results, allowing both a quick “first glance” and rapid zooming in on interesting results.

Fireworks offers an intuitive overview of dataset analysis results in the context of the Reactome hierarchical pathway structure, allowing both a quick “first glance” and rapid zooming in on interesting results.
